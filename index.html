Cat Appreciation Page

Introduction

Greetings, reader of this website. My name is Joy, and I am a fervent and passionate lover of cats. I am dedicated to appreciating these fascinating creatures, and I have created this web application to share with you more about them.

Why do I love cats so much?

Cats have complex yet adorable personalities. They always carry themselves with grace, and know how to keep themselves clean and presentable. Intelligent and perceptive, they approach every situation skillfully and vigilantly.

They may appear arrogant at first glance, but you must put your pride aside and serve these classy felines with your most well-refined petting skills. Only then will you gain their love and affection as their loyal subjects.

Here are some examples of how my love for cats shows in my life.

  • I have 2 cat plushies on my bed
  • I dedicated an entire island in my Genshin Impact Serenitea Pot to cats
